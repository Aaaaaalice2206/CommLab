Welcome, Lungless Lifeform

If you're reading this, you probably don't have lungs.
Maybe you're a ghost, a jellyfish, or a cloud of consciousness. That's okay.

Breathing is more than just a physical act. It's a state of being.

Step 1: Sense the Flow

Close your... whatever you use to sense things.
Feel the flow of universal energy moving through your... you-ness.

Step 2: Inhale in your Imagination

Imagine a breath. You don't need lungs to breathe in:

  • peace
  • calm
  • cosmic vibes

Simply intend to breathe. The universe understands.

Step 3: Exhale the Ego

Let go. Of thoughts. Of form. Of fear.
Breathe out everything you're not. Stay with what remains.

You are now breathing spiritually. Well done.
