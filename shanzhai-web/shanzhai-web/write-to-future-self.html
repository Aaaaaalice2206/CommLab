Write to Your Future Self

You can guide yourself to write a letter to your future self six months from now.
It won't actually be sent, but will disappear automatically after writing,
or it will forever exist somewhere deep in a 404 page,
symbolizing "no one will ever see it".

Write down your most shameful emotions you don't want to say out loud... You don't have to finish it; just let it exist.
