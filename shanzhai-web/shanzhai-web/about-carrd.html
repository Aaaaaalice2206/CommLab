What is Carrd?

Carrd is a simple, free tool for making one-page websites. No coding or design skills needed. It's fast, clean, and perfect for creating quick landing pages, portfolios, or personal sites.

But what if we took that simplicity and added something darker? What if instead of just a clean design, we showed the cracks and raw emotions underneath?

That's where Scarrd comes in. I've twisted Carrd’s simple beauty into something more... real. Something that’s broken, emotional, and honest.
